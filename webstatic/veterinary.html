Veterinary Support Services

Access expert veterinary services across various regions to ensure the health and productivity of your livestock. Find the best practices and contact reliable veterinary professionals near you.

Veterinary Services by Region

Northern Province

The Northern Province is home to livestock-intensive farming communities. Key veterinary services include vaccination drives, artificial insemination, and nutritional consultations. Renowned centers: New Vision Veterinary Hospital Musanze, ISAE animal clinic.

Southern Province

This region specializes in dairy and poultry farming. Services here focus on disease prevention, milk quality testing, and poultry health management. Key providers: VSF Belgium

Eastern Province

The Eastern Province has extensive cattle ranches and grazing fields. Services provided include deworming programs, emergency veterinary care, and breeding assistance. Contact points: Nyagatare Veterinary Hub, Kibungo Animal Care Unit.

Western Province

Known for mixed farming, this region offers support for both small and large-scale farmers. Veterinary services include disease surveillance, vaccinations, and pest control for livestock. Popular providers: Karongi Veterinary Hospital, Rubavu Livestock Clinic.

Kigali City

Kigali serves as a hub for urban farming and small-scale livestock keeping. Services include pet care, livestock diagnostics, and training for urban farmers. Renowned centers: Kacyiru Animal Care Center, Kanombe Veterinary Services.

Best Practices for Livestock Health

  • Regular Health Checkups: Schedule regular veterinary visits to detect and address health issues early.
  • Vaccination Programs: Protect your livestock against common diseases such as foot-and-mouth disease and Newcastle disease.
  • Balanced Nutrition: Provide a well-balanced diet tailored to the specific needs of your animals for optimal growth and productivity.
  • Biosecurity Measures: Prevent the spread of diseases by maintaining clean facilities and controlling animal movements.
  • Proper Housing: Ensure animals have adequate shelter to protect them from harsh weather and reduce stress.
